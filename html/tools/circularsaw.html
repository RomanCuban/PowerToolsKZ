A circular saw is a power tool equipped with a toothed or abrasive disc that rotates rapidly to cut various materials such as wood, metal, plastic, and masonry. It's commonly used in woodworking, construction, and DIY projects for making straight and precise cuts. Circular saws come in different types, including sidewinder (or inline) and worm-drive saws, each with its own advantages in terms of power and handling. They offer adjustable cutting depths and bevel angles for versatile cutting options. Safety features such as blade guards and ergonomic handles are standard to ensure safe operation. Circular saws are essential tools in many workshops and job sites due to their efficiency, accuracy, and versatility.
