The screwdriver is a handheld tool designed for turning screws to either fasten or loosen them. It typically consists of a handle and a shaft ending in a tip that fits into the head of a screw. Screwdrivers come in various sizes and types to accommodate different screw heads, such as flathead, Phillips, or Torx. They are essential tools in carpentry, construction, and household repairs.

A screwdriver manual typically provides instructions on how to use, maintain, and troubleshoot screwdrivers. It covers topics such as different types of screwdrivers, proper handling techniques, safety precautions, and maintenance tips. The manual may also include information on selecting the right screwdriver for specific tasks, understanding screw types and sizes, and common problems encountered when using screwdrivers and how to solve them. Overall, a screwdriver manual serves as a comprehensive guide to help users effectively and safely utilize this essential tool.
